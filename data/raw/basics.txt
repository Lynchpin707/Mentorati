TOPIC: Ser vs Estar (The two verbs for "To Be")

SER (Permanent/Essential characteristics)
- Identity: Soy Lina. (I am Lina.)
- Profession: Soy ingeniera de datos. (I am a data engineer.)
- Origin: Soy de Marruecos. (I am from Morocco.)
- Characteristics: El coche es rápido. (The car is fast.)
- Time/Date: Son las tres. (It is three o’clock.)

ESTAR (Temporary states/Location)
- Position/Location: Estoy en Casablanca. (I am in Casablanca.)
- Action (Present Progressive): Estoy aprendiendo español. (I am learning Spanish.)
- Emotion/Condition: Estoy feliz. (I am happy.)
- Physical state: La sopa está caliente. (The soup is hot.)

TOPIC: Por vs Para (The two words for "For")

POR (Reason, Cause, Through, Exchange)
- Reason/Cause: No fui por la lluvia. (I didn't go because of the rain.)
- Through/Along: Camino por el parque. (I walk through the park.)
- Duration: Estudié por dos horas. (I studied for two hours.)
- Exchange: Te doy 10 euros por el libro. (I give you 10 euros for the book.)

PARA (Purpose, Destination, Deadline, Recipient)
- Purpose (In order to): Estudio para aprender. (I study in order to learn.)
- Destination: Salgo para Madrid. (I am leaving for Madrid.)
- Deadline: La tarea es para el lunes. (The homework is for Monday.)
- Recipient: Este regalo es para ti. (This gift is for you.)
